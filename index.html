About GPLA

The Global Plant Leadership Association (GPLA) is an exclusive network of the world's most accomplished plant managers, operations directors, and manufacturing leaders. Our mission is to advance manufacturing excellence through shared knowledge, innovative practices, and collaborative leadership.


Founded by industry veterans, GPLA brings together leaders from Fortune 500 companies and emerging manufacturers to tackle the most pressing challenges in modern production environments.
